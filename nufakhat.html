Nufakhat Nahrainyah

Al-Nahrain University held a large student festival in its fifth session, entitled "Nufakhat Nahrainyah", and was attended by the University President, Prof. Dr. Ali Abdel Aziz Al-Shawi, the Assistant President for Scientific Affairs, Prof. Dr. Omar Farouk, and many professors and university students to participate in the annual festival organized by the student laughter team. The festival aims to bid farewell to the year 2022 and welcome the new year 2023 by launching balloons loaded with new wishes and dreams, as the proceeds of this festival go to humanitarian aid under the slogan (we rejoice and make people happy). The breadth of its scientific and research presence has given the university high evaluation levels on both levels Local and global, and within a broader quality of Al-Nahrain University. The festival is distinguished by the large turnout and the diversity of the activities presented in it. Between presenting experiences, showing arts and playing music, students show their energies and awareness by educating each other, by presenting several success experiences and scenes of health awareness and education on adherence to preventive measures and offering praise to medical teams, thus forming the highest levels of thought that he possesses The university student and his awareness of the importance of his role within society.

Nahrain University is one of the best accredited universities in Iraq offering programs in: Medicine, Engineering, Science, Information and Communication Engineering and much more.....
